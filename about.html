About Shannon

Leadership Through Complexity

Neuroscientist. Sales leader. Certified coach. 17+ years driving performance in healthcare and biotech — now helping leaders do the same.

Professional Background

Shannon Corley is a sales and business leader with 17+ years of experience in healthcare and biotechnology — and a certified professional coach who has spent her career at the intersection of science, technology, and human performance. Her path is anything but linear: from neuroscience research labs at McGill and the University of Calgary, to guiding dives across Malaysia, Thailand, Egypt, and Mexico, to building high-performing sales organizations across Western Canada.

At Dentsply Sirona, Shannon led regional teams through complex technology adoption challenges — driving 433% growth over four years as Sales Manager of the year in 2019 and 2020. That track record wasn't built on pushing product. It was built on understanding people: what motivates them, what slows them down, and what conditions allow them to do their best work.

Her coaching practice is the natural extension of that career. Shannon brings an MSc in Neuroscience, an MBA in Technology Management from SFU, and certification from Erickson Coaching International. She works with leaders who are navigating real complexity — and who want to grow not just in results, but in how they show up for the people in their charge.

Philosophy

What I Believe

"I spent years studying the brain, then years inside fast-moving organizations watching how people perform under pressure. The science and the lived experience point to the same thing: performance is a conditions problem before it's a people problem."

"The best leaders I've worked with don't have more answers than everyone else. They ask better questions. They create space for people to think clearly, take risks, and bring their best. That's the work."

"Leadership is about taking care of the people in your charge. Everything else — results, growth, strategy — follows from getting that right."
